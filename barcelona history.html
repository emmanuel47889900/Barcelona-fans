Legends of the Club

FC Barcelona has been graced by some of the greatest players to ever step on a pitch: Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernández, Andrés Iniesta, Ronaldinho, Carles Puyol, Johan Cruyff, Rivaldo, and many more.

Trophy Cabinet

Barça boasts an incredible collection of silverware: 27 La Liga titles, 31 Copa del Reys, 5 UEFA Champions League trophies, 3 FIFA Club World Cups, 14 Spanish Super Cups, 4 UEFA Cup Winners' Cups, and 5 UEFA Super Cups.
